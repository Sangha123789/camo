Article: What is Democracy?


Democracy is a form of government that allows people to choose their rulers. In a democracy, only leaders elected by people should rule the country and community.

Democracy allows ordinary people to have a say in what the government does. People have the freedom to express views, freedom to organise and freedom to protest. Adults over 18 can make decisions about their everyday lives.

The most common type of democracy is called representative democracy.
