About Camolitics

Camolitics offers articles and video content aiming to share knowledge on democratic values and responsible citizenship.

Camolitics shares educational content related to democracy, elections, political parties, justice, civil society, and human rights.

Camolitics believes that civic education is a prerequisite for a well-functioning democracy. New generations should have a thorough understanding of the democratic system and an appreciation of the values that are meant to govern our society.
